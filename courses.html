Courses¶

The VM combines various software packages, course materials and sample data.

The courses are based on IPython notebook. The material has been evolving since the very first “Open Source Radar Short Course” at ERAD 2014. Since then, several courses have been carried out at ERAD and AMS meeting as well as other occasions.

See here how to start a jupyter server and access the course material on the VM.

You can also access the repositories with course materials directly:

  • Py-ART course
  • wradlib course
  • BALTRAD course
  • BALTRAD 2 wradlib interoperability demo
  • Py-ART 2 BALTRAD interoperability demo
  • Radx course (as yet very brief)
  • PyTMatrix course
